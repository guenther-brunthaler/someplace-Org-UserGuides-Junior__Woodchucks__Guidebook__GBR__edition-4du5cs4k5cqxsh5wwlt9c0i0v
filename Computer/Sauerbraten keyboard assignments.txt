Sauerbraten-Tasten
* E edit mode
* "`" or "T"-"/" for console
* Selecting cubes lets you edit many at once and you don't have to point to edit them
* Jump to deselect
* Use the mouse wheel to pull cubes into existence and push them out of existence
* Q+Mouse Wheel to push and pull a corner
* F+Mouse Wheel to push and pull all 4 corners at once
* R+Mouse Wheel to rotate
* Y+Mouse Wheel to texture
* G+Mouse Wheel to change grid size
* U to undo one step
* X to mirror relative to the side of the white box
* /savemap mapname_optional to save your map
* /newmap 7 creates a new map of size class 7
* /map mapname loads a map
